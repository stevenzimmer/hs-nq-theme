What is Linear Regression?

Linear regression is used in clinical trials to evaluate the relationship between two continuous variables. The goal of linear regression is often to model the relationship between an independent variable (such as a treatment or a risk factor) and a dependent variable (such as a patient outcome or a biomarker).
